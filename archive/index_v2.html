Elected Officer Positions

Select a position below to understand its core responsibilities before you nominate yourself. Elected officers are expected to serve for at least one semester.

📅

Events Officer

  • Lead a team of 2-3 to plan Meet the Firms and other major events.
  • Manage logistics, vendors, budget, and RSVPs.
  • Coordinate with relations officers for promotion.
🎯

Activities Officer

  • Lead a team of 1-2 students.
  • Schedule and logically manage bi-weekly presentations, open houses, and class visits.
🤝

Firm Relations Officer

  • Communicate directly with accounting firms regarding upcoming events.
  • Manage firm responses and execute follow-up communications.
🏢

CAS & Industry Relations Officer

  • Connect industry organizations with UVU students.
  • Collaborate with David Waite to develop industry relationships.
  • Manage communications and follow-ups with industry/government orgs.
📣

Student & Faculty Relations Officer

  • Lead a team of 1-2 students.
  • Distribute event info to students and faculty.
  • Update display screens, elevators, and maintain the public UVAS calendar.
🏆

Competitions Officer

  • Lead a team of 1-2 students.
  • Organize case studies and academic competitions, including inviting other universities.
📚

Historian

  • Record meeting minutes and track event attendance.
  • Maintain a historical record of all UVAS activities.
  • Provide updates to the Relations Officer for the website.

About UVAS

The Utah Valley Accounting Society connects students with the profession, facilitating firm visits, networking, and resume recruitment. We work closely with the UACPA and AICPA to build your career network.

Why Join Leadership?

  • Direct networking with top firms
  • Leadership experience for your resume
  • Department involvement and impact
